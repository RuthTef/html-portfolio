The Best Albums According to Me

These are my top 3 favorite Albums of right now!

In the Key of Life - Stevie Wonder

This is my favorite album of all time! As is my favorite song of all time for sure.

The Miseducation of Lauryn Hill

There are so many good songs off this album. The mixing of afrosoul with hip hop and rap is amazing.

To Pimp a Butterfly - K Dot

This was such a raw interspective of being black and poor in America. And the deconstruction process was amazing.
